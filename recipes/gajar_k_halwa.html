Gajar ka Halwa

Description:-

CAUTION:This Indian dish is higly addictive in taste try it on your own risk.

Gajar ka Halwa, a beloved Indian dessert, is a luscious concoction of grated carrots slow-cooked in ghee, simmered with milk, and sweetened to perfection, offering a comforting and aromatic treat

Ingredients:

  • 500g fresh carrots, grated
  • 1/4 cup ghee (clarified butter)
  • 1 cup full-cream milk
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup chopped nuts (almonds, cashews, pistachios)
  • 1/2 tbsp caradamom powder

Steps

  1. Grating carrots:
    Grate fresh carrots, preparing the foundation for the delectable Gajar ka Halwa.
  2. Cooking in ghee:
    Saut√© the grated carrots in ghee until they turn tender and release their aromatic essence.
  3. Simmering with Milk and Sweetening:
    Add milk and sugar, letting the mixture simmer until the carrots absorb sweetness. Finish by adding cardamom powder and garnishing with chopped nuts. Serve warm, savoring the richness of Gajar ka Halwa
